Features

Built for the field

Every feature is designed for real disaster response — fast, reliable, and built for people who don't have time to waste.

🗺️
Live Crisis Dashboard
Real-time Firestore feed of active reports. Sort by nearest, latest, or most urgent. Haversine distance from volunteer's GPS. Completed tasks sink to the bottom.
✦
Gemini AI Analysis
Every report is analyzed instantly — situation summary, 3 actionable solutions, required skills, estimated people affected, and priority level before dispatch.
📋
Field Report Submission
Geo-tagged reports with issue type (Food / Medical / Shelter), urgency level, auto-fetched GPS coordinates, and up to 5 photo or video attachments.
🙋
Structured Volunteer Flow
4-stage workflow: Assigned → En Route → On Site → Done. 1km geofence confirms arrival. Completion requires photo proof stored in Firebase Storage.
👤
Verified NGO Volunteers
Role-based access — only verified NGO volunteers can accept tasks. Profiles track reports submitted, tasks accepted, completed, and success rate.
⚙️
Emergency Hotlines & Settings
Quick-dial for Police (100), Ambulance (102), Fire (101). Dark/light mode toggle, notifications, language and privacy controls.

How It Works

Report to resolution

A structured end-to-end pipeline — from the moment a citizen files a crisis report to a volunteer submitting photo proof of completion.

STEP 01
File a Report
Citizen captures the crisis with geo-tag, issue type, urgency, and photos — done in seconds.
STEP 02
Gemini Analyzes
AI surfaces urgency, required skills, actionable solutions, and estimated impact instantly.
STEP 03
Volunteer Accepts
Verified NGO volunteers see it on the live dashboard and accept the nearest task.
STEP 04
Navigate & Assist
Google Maps navigation. 1km geofence automatically confirms on-site arrival.
STEP 05
Submit Proof
Photo proof + note uploaded to Firebase Storage. Task marked done.
